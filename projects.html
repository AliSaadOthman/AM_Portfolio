Trip Global

A sophisticated web application developed for Trip Global, a company specializing in fly fishing equipment and fishing services. This application employs advanced scalable techniques and leverages powerful frontend and backend frameworks, React and Node.js. It features a robust database for efficient data storage, ensuring readiness for future AI solutions. The application delivers a seamless user experience, combining cutting-edge technology with Trip Global's industry expertise.

Colin Candy

Colin Candy, a vibrant candy shop, wanted to develop a user-friendly web application that reflects their playful and joyful spirit, we developed a custom-built, responsive website that not only showcases Colin Candy's wide range of confectionery delights but also enhances user engagement through interactive elements and a seamless shopping experience. Customers can easily navigate the website, explore product offerings, and make purchases securely. Integration of Colin's character throughout the website adds a whimsical charm, reinforcing brand identity and fostering customer loyalty.
